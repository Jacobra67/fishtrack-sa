📋 Printing Instructions

For Best Results:

  • Print at 300 DPI minimum
  • Recommended sticker size: 3" x 3" (75mm x 75mm)
  • Use weatherproof vinyl sticker paper
  • Test scan QR code before mass printing

🎯 Where to Place:

  • Tackle shop windows & counters
  • Boat launch slipways
  • Fishing pier notice boards
  • Popular beach access points
  • Bait & tackle boxes (with permission)
