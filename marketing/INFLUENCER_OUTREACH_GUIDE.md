# FishTrack Africa - Influencer Outreach Guide
## How to Partner with SA Fishing Influencers

**Goal:** 10-20 influencer partnerships in 6 months → 2,000+ installs

---

## Why Influencer Marketing Works for FishTrack

**1. Built-in Trust:**
- Followers trust their recommendations
- You're a stranger, they're a friend
- "If Dean uses it, I'll use it"

**2. Targeted Audience:**
- 100% fishing enthusiasts
- Already interested in fishing tech/apps
- Higher conversion than random ads

**3. Social Proof:**
- "10K people follow this guy, he knows fishing"
- Credibility transfer (he's legit → FishTrack is legit)

**4. Cost-Effective:**
- Micro-influencers: R500-2,000 per post
- Reach: 5K-50K highly targeted people
- CPM (cost per 1,000 views): R10-40 (vs R50-100 for ads)

## The 5-Step Influencer Outreach Process

### Step 1: Research & Shortlist (1 hour)

**Where to Find Them:**
- TikTok: Search #SAFishing, #CapeFishing, #FishingLifestyle
- Instagram: Search same hashtags
- YouTube: "South Africa fishing"
- Facebook: Fishing groups (admins are often influencers)

**Vetting Checklist:**
- Follower count matches engagement? (1K followers but 10 likes = fake)
- SA-based? (you want local audience)
- Active? (posted in last 7 days)
- Audience fits? (fishing, not random)
- Content quality? (do they post regularly?)

**Create Spreadsheet:**
| Name | Platform | Followers | Engagement | Contact | Notes |
|------|----------|-----------|------------|---------|-------|
| Dean D | TikTok | 30K | 3% | @fishsa | Reached out ✅ |

---

### Step 2: Personalized Outreach (5 min per influencer)

**BAD Outreach (Generic):**
```
Hey! Want to partner with FishTrack? We're a fishing app.
```

**GOOD Outreach (Personalized):**
```
Hey Kyle,

Massive fan - that Henties Bay vid with the 20kg Bronze Whaler was insane! 🦈

I'm Cobie, a shore angler from Paarl. Built FishTrack Africa to help our community 
see where fish are biting (like Strava for fishing).

100% free, no ads. Community-driven (anglers log catches, everyone benefits).

Would you be keen to try it? If you like it, maybe feature it in a vid?

Happy to give you early access + custom features for your crew.

Tight lines,
Cobie

P.S. Your editing skills are next level. What do you use?
```

**Why This Works:**
1. Personalized (mentioned specific video)
2. Introduced yourself (fellow angler, not salesperson)
3. Value prop clear (helps anglers)
4. No ask yet (just "try it")
5. Soft CTA ("if you like it")
6. P.S. builds rapport (genuine question)

---

### Step 3: Follow-Up (If No Response After 3-7 Days)

**Follow-Up Message:**
```
Hey Kyle,

Following up on my last message about FishTrack.

Just launched this week - already 50 anglers logging catches across SA/Namibia.

Here's a quick demo: [30-second video link showing app]

Would love your feedback (even if you're not interested in partnering).

Cheers,
Cobie
```

**Why:** Most people miss first message. Follow-up doubles response rate.

---

### Step 4: The Partnership Pitch (After They Respond)

**If They Say "Tell me more":**

**Phase 1 Pitch (Free Value Exchange):**
```
Awesome! Here's the idea:

1. You try FishTrack for free
2. Log a few catches (takes 30 seconds)
3. If you like it, feature it in a video/post

What's in it for you:
- Early access (you're part of building it)
- Custom features (if you want something, I'll build it)
- Lifetime premium when we launch paid tier (R99/month value)
- Your audience discovers better fishing spots (they'll thank you)

What's in it for me:
- Feedback from a pro angler
- Exposure to your audience
- Help build the SA fishing community

No pressure - try it first, see if it's useful.

Want me to send you the link?
```

**Phase 2 Pitch (Paid Partnership - Month 4+):**
```
Hey Kyle,

FishTrack is growing fast (1,000+ users now!). Your followers are loving it.

Want to do an official partnership?

Offer:
- R1,500 for a dedicated video (60 seconds)
- You show how you use FishTrack on a fishing trip
- Authentic integration (not salesy)
- We promote your video on our socials (free exposure)

I'll send you talking points, but you make it your own style.

Interested?
```

---

### Step 5: Nurture the Relationship (Ongoing)

**Don't Ghost After One Post:**
- Comment on their posts (build rapport)
- Share their content (give them reach)
- Send updates ("We hit 1,000 users! Thanks for your support")
- Feature them on FishTrack ("Influencer Spotlight: Kyle Christie")

**Why:** Long-term relationships > one-off posts

## Content Guidelines (What to Brief Them)

**Dos:**
✅ Show you actually using the app (authentic)
✅ Explain what FishTrack does (benefits, not features)
✅ Share a real catch you logged
✅ Use natural language ("I use this to find spots")
✅ Include clear CTA ("Link in bio")

**Don'ts:**
❌ Scripted/robotic ("FishTrack Africa is an application...")
❌ Over-promise ("You'll catch 10x more fish!")
❌ Generic stock footage (use your own content)
❌ No clear CTA (viewers don't know what to do)

**Example Brief:**
```
Video Concept:
- Show a fishing trip where you catch fish
- "I found this spot on FishTrack" (show app)
- "Other anglers logged catches here, so I tried it"
- Show the catch
- "Just logged mine - now others can find this spot"
- CTA: "Link in bio to download FishTrack"

Keep it natural, your style. ~60 seconds.
```

## Tracking ROI (Is It Working?)

**Metrics to Track:**

**1. Reach:**
- How many people saw the post? (views)

**2. Engagement:**
- Likes, comments, shares (did people care?)

**3. Traffic:**
- Use unique URLs: `fishtrack.co.za?ref=kyle`
- Track in Google Analytics

**4. Installs:**
- How many downloaded after seeing post?
- Ask influencer for analytics screenshot

**5. Cost Per Install:**
- Paid R2,000 → 200 installs = R10 per install
- Goal: <R20 per install

**Good ROI:**
- Micro-influencer: 50-200 installs per post
- Macro-influencer: 200-1,000 installs per post

## Red Flags (Bad Influencers to Avoid)

### 🚩 Fake Followers:
- 10K followers but 50 likes per post
- Comments are generic: "Nice! 🔥🔥"
- Use SocialBlade to check (sudden follower spikes = bought)

### 🚩 Engagement Pods:
- Same 20 people comment on every post
- Reciprocal engagement ("I like yours, you like mine")

### 🚩 Irrelevant Audience:
- Fishing account but followers are interested in fashion
- International audience (you need SA anglers)

### 🚩 Unprofessional:
- Doesn't respond to messages
- Misses deadlines
- Posts without approval

**How to Vet:** Ask for media kit (reach, demographics, engagement rate)
