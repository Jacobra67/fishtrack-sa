## TIPS FOR FILMING:

**DO:**
✅ Film in landscape (phone sideways)
✅ Keep phone stable (prop it or use someone to hold)
✅ Speak clearly but naturally
✅ Film multiple takes (you'll choose best in edit)
✅ Film MORE than you think you need

**DON'T:**
❌ Film in portrait (vertical) - harder to edit
❌ Film in dark lighting
❌ Film with loud background noise (traffic, wind tunnel)
❌ Rush through screen recording (go SLOW)
❌ Stress about perfection
