# FishTrack Africa - Video Production Guide
**For someone who's NEVER made a YouTube video before**

---

## DON'T PANIC!

You don't need:
- ❌ Expensive camera (phone is perfect)
- ❌ Professional editing software (free apps work great)
- ❌ Acting skills (just be yourself)
- ❌ Perfect takes (authenticity > polish)

You DO need:
- ✅ Your phone
- ✅ 2-3 hours over a few days
- ✅ Willingness to try
- ✅ Your normal fishing vibe

---

## PHASE 1: PRE-PRODUCTION (30 minutes - TODAY)

### Step 1: Read the Script
- Open `youtube-video-script.md`
- Read it OUT LOUD 2-3 times
- Change any words that don't sound like YOU
- Add Afrikaans where it feels natural
- Make it conversational, not scripted

### Step 2: Plan Your Shots
You need 3 types of footage:
1. **You talking** (beach/dam/home, doesn't matter)
2. **Screen recording** (showing the app)
3. **B-roll** (optional: waves, fishing gear, sunset - makes it pretty)

### Step 3: Pick Your Location
- **Easy option:** Your backyard/home (good enough!)
- **Better option:** Beach/dam (authentic fishing vibe)
- **Lighting:** Daytime, natural light, sun behind camera (not behind you)
- **Sound:** Quiet-ish (wind is okay, traffic noise isn't)

---

## PHASE 2: RECORDING (1-2 hours - DAY 2)

### Part A: You Talking (30-45 min)

**Setup:**
1. Prop phone against something stable (books, rocks, tackle box)
2. Or ask someone to hold it
3. Frame: You from waist up, centered
4. Check lighting (can you see your face clearly?)
5. Do a 10-second test recording, watch it back

**Recording Tips:**
- Look at the camera lens, not the screen
- Speak like you're talking to a mate, not a boardroom
- Don't worry about mistakes - you can edit or re-record
- Do the WHOLE script in one go, even if you mess up
- Then re-record just the parts you messed up
- Better to have extra footage than not enough

**What to Record:**
- Hook (beach/dam intro)
- Problem section (can do this at home)
- Solution walkthrough (hold phone, show app while talking)
- Comparison section (home is fine)
- Call to action (beach/dam, show QR code)
- Outro (walking away shot - optional but cool)

### Part B: Screen Recording (15-30 min)

**On Android:**
1. Pull down notification shade
2. Look for "Screen Record" tile (or search in settings)
3. If not there: Download "AZ Screen Recorder" (free, no watermark)

**On iPhone:**
1. Settings → Control Center → Add Screen Recording
2. Swipe down, tap record button

**What to Record:**
1. Open FishTrack Africa homepage (5 seconds)
2. Navigate to Log Catch (10 seconds)
3. Fill in form slowly (30 seconds)
4. Show Secret Spot privacy options (15 seconds, HIGHLIGHT THIS)
5. Drop pin on map (10 seconds)
6. Click Auto-fill conditions (10 seconds, show it populate)
7. Submit catch (5 seconds)
8. Open Map view (5 seconds)
9. Show clustering (zoom in/out, 10 seconds)
10. Use filters (species, date, 15 seconds)
11. Click a catch, show popup (10 seconds)

**Pro tip:** Do it SLOWLY. Viewers need time to see what you're doing.

### Part C: B-Roll (Optional, 10 min)

**Nice-to-have shots:**
- Waves crashing
- Fishing rod/tackle
- You casting (even if you don't catch anything)
- Sunrise/sunset
- Fish in bucket (if you have one)

**Don't stress if you skip this!** B-roll makes it prettier, but it's not required.

## PHASE 3: EDITING (1-2 hours - DAY 3)

### Easy Option: CapCut (Free, Works Great)

**Download:**
- Android: Play Store → "CapCut"
- iPhone: App Store → "CapCut"

**Basic Editing Steps:**

1. **Import Footage**
   - Open CapCut
   - Tap "New Project"
   - Select all your video clips
   - They'll appear in timeline

2. **Arrange Clips**
   - Drag clips into order (follow script structure)
   - You talking → Screen recording → You talking → etc.

3. **Cut Out Mistakes**
   - Tap clip → Tap "Edit"
   - Drag edges to trim
   - Or tap "Split" to cut out middle sections
   - Delete the bad takes

4. **Add Text Overlays** (Important!)
   - When you say "Secret Spot mode" → Add text: "🔒 SECRET SPOT"
   - When you say "Free forever" → Add text: "FREE FOREVER"
   - When showing features → Add text: "Auto-fill Conditions ✅"
   - Tap "Text" → Type → Choose font → Position on screen

5. **Add Music** (Optional)
   - CapCut has free music library
   - Pick something upbeat but not annoying
   - Keep volume LOW (30%) - your voice is primary

6. **Ending Screen**
   - Last 5 seconds: Add QR code image
   - Add text: "fishtrack-sa.netlify.app"
   - Add text: "FREE FOREVER"

7. **Export**
   - Tap Export
   - Choose 1080p (HD)
   - Save to phone

---

## PHASE 4: THUMBNAIL (15 min - DAY 3)

**Easy Option: Canva (Free)**

1. Open Canva app/website
2. Search template: "YouTube Thumbnail"
3. Pick one you like
4. Change text to: **"SECRET SPOT MODE 🔒"**
5. Add photo of you holding fish (or just you on beach)
6. Make text BIG and BOLD (needs to be readable on small screens)
7. Export as PNG

**Pro tip:** Yellow/red text on dark background = clickable

---

## PHASE 5: UPLOAD (15 min - DAY 4)

1. **YouTube Studio** (on phone or computer)
   - Go to YouTube.com → Sign in
   - Click Camera icon → "Upload video"

2. **Upload Your Video**
   - Select the edited video file
   - While uploading, fill in details:

3. **Title:**
   ```
   This FREE App Protects Your Secret Fishing Spots (FishTrack Africa)
   ```

4. **Description:**
   ```
   Tired of giving away your secret fishing spots every time you log a catch? 

   FishTrack Africa is the first fishing app built FOR South African and Namibian anglers, with SECRET SPOT MODE that lets you share catches publicly without revealing your exact GPS coordinates.

   ✅ Free forever (no ads, no premium BS)
   ✅ Secret Spot privacy mode (2km fuzzy area)
   ✅ Auto-fill conditions (tide, wind, water temp)
   ✅ SA/Namibia species (Galjoen, Steenbras, Kabeljou, etc.)
   ✅ Saltwater + Freshwater (bass, carp, sharks, gamefish)
   ✅ Smart map clustering
   ✅ PWA - works on any phone, no app store needed

   Get started: https://fishtrack-sa.netlify.app

   Built by anglers, for anglers. 🎣🇿🇦

   #FishingApp #SouthAfrica #SecretSpot #Galjoen #BassFishing
   ```

5. **Thumbnail:**
   - Upload the thumbnail you made in Canva

6. **Tags:**
   ```
   fishing app, south africa fishing, secret fishing spots, galjoen, steenbras, bass fishing, fishing tracker
   ```

7. **Publish!**
