## The TikTok Algorithm (How to Win)

### What TikTok Wants:
1. **Watch Time:** People watch your full video (don't skip)
2. **Engagement:** Likes, comments, shares, saves
3. **Rewatches:** People watch it again
4. **Profile Visits:** They want to see more
5. **Follows:** They subscribe to your content

### How to Optimize:
- **Hook in 1 second:** Don't waste time with intros
- **Keep it short:** 15-30 seconds ideal (60 max)
- **End with question:** Forces comments ("What's your PB galjoen?")
- **Trending sounds:** Use popular music (algorithm boost)
- **Captions ON:** 50% watch without sound
- **Post consistently:** 1-2/day minimum

## The 7 Video Formats That Work

### 1. Catch Reveal (Highest Engagement)

**Hook Examples:**
- "You won't believe what I just caught..."
- "This fish almost broke my rod..."
- "POV: You hook your PB"
- "Wait for it... 😱"

**Structure:**
1. Tight line (suspense)
2. Rod bending
3. Flash of fish in water
4. Reveal (face or full body)
5. "Logged on FishTrack!"

**Music:** Epic/suspense tracks (search "epic reveal")

**CTA:** "Link in bio to see where I caught this"

**Example Script:**
```
[Video: Tight line, water splashing]
Text on screen: "Something BIG just hit..."
[Rod bending hard]
Text: "This might be a PB..."
[Fish breaches water - GALJOEN]
Text: "MONSTER GALJOEN! 🐟"
[Show fish closeup]
Text: "Struisbaai Beach - logged on FishTrack"
[Link in bio graphic]
Voice: "Comment 'FishTrack' for the link!"
```

---

### 2. Before & After (Satisfying)

**Hook:** "From this... to THIS"

**Examples:**
- Empty cooler → Full of fish
- Sunrise (no fish) → Sunset (limit caught)
- Bait on hook → Fish in hand
- Tide out (no action) → Tide in (bite is on)

**Why It Works:** Humans love transformations

**FishTrack Integration:**
- "All catches logged on FishTrack"
- Show app at end (map with pins)

---

### 3. Spot Reviews (Educational)

**Hook:** "[Location Name] Fishing - Is it worth it?"

**Structure:**
1. Show the spot (scenic shot)
2. "I've caught [X] fish here"
3. Species breakdown
4. Best conditions (tide, wind)
5. "See all catches on FishTrack"

**Example:**
```
"Struisbaai Beach - The TRUTH"
- Caught: Galjoen, Steenbras, White Musselcracker
- Best: Incoming tide, SE wind, redbait
- 50+ catches logged on FishTrack
- Link in bio for live data
```

**Why It Works:** Value + Call-to-action

---

### 4. Fishing Tips (Build Authority)

**Hook:** "3 Secrets to Catch More Galjoen"

**Format:**
1. Tip #1 (3 seconds)
2. Tip #2 (3 seconds)
3. Tip #3 (3 seconds)
4. "Track your catches on FishTrack"

**Example Topics:**
- "Best bait for [species]"
- "How to read the surf for fish"
- "3 knots every angler needs"
- "Tide timing for shore fishing"
- "How to find fishing spots (use FishTrack!)"

**Why It Works:** Educational content = saves = algorithm boost

---

### 5. Community Highlights (User-Generated Content)

**Hook:** "This Week's Top 3 Catches on FishTrack"

**Structure:**
1. #3: User photo + stats
2. #2: User photo + stats
3. #1: User photo + stats (HUGE fish)
4. "Log yours on FishTrack"

**Example:**
```
#3: James - 5.2kg Steenbras, Struisbaai
#2: Chris - 18kg Bronze Whaler, Henties Bay
#1: Adriaan - 9.8kg Galjoen, Stilbaai (NEW RECORD!)

Comment "Congrats!" below ⬇️
```

**Why It Works:**
- Social proof (people are using it)
- Recognition (users share it = free reach)
- FOMO (they want to be featured)

### 6. Day in the Life (Storytelling)

## Pro Tips (From Viral Creators)

1. **Film horizontally, crop to vertical** (better framing)
2. **Natural light only** (sunrise/sunset = golden hour)
3. **Stabilize with cheap gimbal** (DJI Osmo Mobile, R1,500)
4. **Text on screen** (50% watch without sound)
5. **Hook in first frame** (no "Hey guys, today I'm...")
6. **End with CTA** ("Comment FishTrack for the link!")
7. **Batch record** (10 videos in one session)
8. **Post consistently** (algorithm favors active creators)
