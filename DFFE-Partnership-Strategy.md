## 🎯 Why DFFE Needs FishTrack's Data

### Current Challenges They Face:

1. **Declining Fish Stocks:**
   - Abalone: "heavily depleted" due to poaching
   - Line fish: "heavily depleted to optimal" (varies by species)
   - West Coast Rock Lobster: showing recovery but still monitored
   - Anchovy: lowest level in 15 years
   - Many species status: "unknown" (limited data)

2. **Limited Recreational Fishing Data:**
   - ±300,000 recreational permits issued annually
   - Estimated 17,000 tons caught per year
   - **BUT: Very limited data on WHERE, WHEN, and WHAT species**
   - No real-time monitoring of recreational catches

3. **Resource Management Issues:**
   - Illegal fishing (poaching) is rampant
   - Climate change affecting fish populations
   - Need better data to set quotas and bag limits
   - User conflicts between commercial and recreational sectors

**FishTrack collects:**
- ✅ **Species caught** (45 species database)

**Why this matters:**
- DFFE struggles to monitor 300,000 recreational fishers

## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

### Risk 1: Government moves slowly
**Mitigation:** Start informal relationship, don't wait for formal MOU

### Risk 2: Data privacy concerns
**Mitigation:** Anonymize all data (no user names, only aggregate stats)

### Risk 3: Conflict with commercial fishers
**Mitigation:** Position as "recreational only," not competing with commercial

### Risk 4: DFFE wants exclusive data rights
**Mitigation:** Negotiate shared ownership, not exclusive handover
